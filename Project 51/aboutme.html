About Origami Studio.

Origami Studio was an initatitve made by Honey. We started as a small project for a college art assignment. But soon, Origami Studio had 2 members, Honey's friends. Slowly,it grew and by the time Honey graduated, Origami Studio had 30 members and 15 frequent customers. Now, Origami Studio is a forum for Origami artists to sell and share their work. It is also used for birthday parties and learning the Japenese art of Origami through proffessionals!
